How Bardo works

Bardo's mission is to help groups of friends discover new music that they all enjoy.

Music recommendations

Bardo has 2 main components: the Spotify API and the Bardo AI:

  • Bardo uses the Spotify API to create music playlists, based on a combination of music similar to the group's preferences and random music from given genres.

  • The Bardo AI learns from the music preferences that members of a group share with Bardo, and hand-picks the actual music to recommend, from the Spotify playlists. As more music is shared with Bardo, Bardo makes better recommendations.

Giving feedback to Bardo

Bardo needs to learn a group's preferences to improve its recommendations. There are 2 ways of doing this:

  • After generating a recommendations playlist, Bardo will ask for feedback. This must be completed before requesting more recommendations.
  • Group members can directly feed their music preferences from their Spotify accounts in their Bardo Profile.
