How Bardo works

Bardo's mission is to help groups of friends find new music that they all enjoy.

The main idea behind Bardo has 2 steps:

  1. Bardo makes recommendations, based on the music preferences that it knows about you.
  2. You rate those recommendations, so that Bardo continues learning. It's an iterative process.

A second idea has to do with groups of friends. If you and your friends have similar music preferences, it makes sense to cross-pollinate them. In other words, the music preferences of your friends might feed into your recommendations and viceversa.
